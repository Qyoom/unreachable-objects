Effulgence (Grid Fan #1)

This piece represents a welcome affirmation of my exploratory process so far. In previous experiments I have been exploring how to do simple rotation of grid cells in sequence variations. My initial target was to emulate the classic 1968 - 1970 piece by George Nees.[1] During such investigation of artists and their work I hope to make discoveries and draw inspiration from experiments, accidents, and coincidences such as this along the way.

[1] https://collections.vam.ac.uk/item/O221321/schotter-print-nees-georg/ Victoria and Albert Museum.
