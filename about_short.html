About

My name is Richard Walker. I’m a coder, visualizer, writer, and wonderer. This site is where I’m “showing my work”[1] as I experiment with making generative art[2], puzzles, and games. This exploration entails study of programming languages and algorithms as well as learning from the work of other artists and creative coders. Over time I hope my process of reaching allows me to contribute to the community of coders and creators in the sharing ideas, inspiration, and feedback.

[1] Kleon, Austin. Show Your Work: 10 Ways to Share Your Creativity and Get Discovered. Workman Publishing Company, 2014.

[2] Jason Bailey, Why Love Generative Art? August 26, 2018
